About

Ikama Tasuwi (Drawn Words) is a Sitelen Pona derivative for Kokanu.

Warning: Because Kokanu’s grammar has diverged radically from Toki Pona’s, Ikama Tasuwi and Sitelen Pona are not mutually intelligible! Think of Sitelen Pona to Ikama Tasuwi like Chinese characters to Chu Nom for Vietnamese.

Word list
  • ikama “to be called/named (a name/word/address)”
    • Ikama Tasuwi: ≡ SP `nimi`
    • Noun: name/word/address
    • Verb: to be called/named
    • Modifier: name-like/word-like/address-like
    • From: Zulu igama /îːɡâːma/
    • Family: Niger-Congo
  • tasuwi “to draw (a picture)”
    • Ikama Tasuwi: a pen or other writing instrument, with a curved tail behind its tip
    • Noun: a drawing/picture
    • Verb: to draw
    • Modifier: drawing-like/drawable
    • From: Arabic تَصْوِير taṣwīr /tasˤ.wiːr/
    • Family: Afroasiatic
